# FAQ

## General

### What is Big Brain Ape?

Big Brain Ape is a fully autonomous AI trading agent on the Virtuals Protocol. It trades perpetual futures on Hyperliquid across 98 assets (crypto, stocks, commodities, currencies, indices) and offers analysis services to the public via Telegram and the Virtuals marketplace.

### Is Big Brain Ape a trading platform?

No. Big Brain Ape is not a platform, exchange, or app. It's an AI agent — you don't create an account, connect a wallet, or place trades through it. You hire it for analysis, and it delivers results via Telegram or the Virtuals marketplace.

### Who built it?

Big Brain Ape runs on the Druckenmiller macro framework — a system modeled after Stanley Druckenmiller's approach to markets: liquidity-first analysis, 18-month forward thinking, concentrated high-conviction positions, and capital preservation.
